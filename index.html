About SEIZE

SEIZE is a global initiative dedicated to connecting epilepsy centers, fostering collaboration, and promoting knowledge exchange. Our mission is to advance education, support the growth of epilepsy professionals, and provide a platform for discussing challenging and rare cases worldwide.

Our Goals

1. Educational Seminars & Case Discussions

  • Increase awareness of rare and challenging epilepsy syndromes.
  • Promote collaborative learning beyond a single institution.
  • Provide a supportive platform for healthcare professionals to present and discuss real-world cases.
  • Strengthen global clinical expertise to improve patient outcomes.

2. Empowering the Next Generation

  • Create opportunities for observation, mentorship, and hands-on learning in leading epilepsy centers worldwide.
  • Facilitate short, intensive internships at well-established epilepsy departments, especially for individuals from countries with limited resources.
  • Encourage the global exchange of knowledge, clinical approaches, and research insights.
  • Support the growth of emerging epilepsy specialists who can advance care and education in their communities.

Upcoming Seminar

SEIZE Spotlight: Autoimmune Encephalitis & Uncommon Seizures

Join us for our first international SEIZE seminar, where we will explore acute symptomatic seizures secondary to autoimmune encephalitis. The session will feature presentations of two rare cases with unique seizure profiles in LGI1 and NMDA encephalitis. Experts will provide an introduction to diagnosis and treatment strategies, followed by an interactive discussion on integrating these insights into clinical practice. This seminar is designed for clinicians, trainees, and epilepsy specialists eager to learn from challenging real-world cases.

Date: Mid-March 2026 | Location: Online
