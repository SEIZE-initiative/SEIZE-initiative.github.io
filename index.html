Connecting centers, solving cases

About SEIZE

SEIZE is a global initiative dedicated to connecting epilepsy centers, fostering collaboration, and promoting knowledge exchange. Our mission is to advance education, support the growth of epilepsy professionals, and provide a platform for discussing challenging and rare cases worldwide.

Our Goals

Educational Seminars & Case Discussions

  • Increase awareness of rare and complex epilepsy syndromes
  • Promote collaboration across institutions
  • Encourage discussion of real-world clinical cases
  • Improve global patient outcomes

Empowering the Next Generation

  • Support mentorship and training opportunities
  • Enable international clinical exposure
  • Encourage knowledge exchange
  • Strengthen future epilepsy leaders
