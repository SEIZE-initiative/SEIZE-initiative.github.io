About SEIZE

SEIZE is an international initiative created to foster collaborative learning across epilepsy centers worldwide. Complex or rare seizure cases—especially those involving subtle imaging findings, uncommon etiologies, or challenging clinical decisions—benefit from discussion beyond a single institution.

SEIZE provides a global, case-based forum where centers can share experience, exchange perspectives, and learn from one another. By promoting open dialogue and real-world clinical discussion, SEIZE aims to reduce institutional silos and strengthen collective expertise in epilepsy care.
